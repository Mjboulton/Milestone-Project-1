About

Mclaren Dance Company has been established since 1978, offering Elite training in graded dance classes for exams, open group dance classes and private lessons at the studio in Rawtenstall and teaching a high standard of R.A.D Ballet exams and N.A.T.D Tap Exams with ninety percent of pupils gaining distinction. We teach all aspects and styles of dance for all age groups, and many of our pupils who have trained with us have gone on to dance professionally. We are sure that we will have a class to suit you.
